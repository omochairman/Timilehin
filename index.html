CHRISTMAS SALES ANALYSIS

ONYX CHRISTMAS SALES DATA CHALLENGE?

Project Overview

The dataset comprises Christmas sales that occurred between November and December from the years 2018 to 2023.

The primary goal of the analysis was to examine trends in Christmas sales and evaluate the impact of various events and promotions on sales performance. Additionally, the analysis aimed to address the issue of return rates, investigating the factors contributing to high return rates during this festive period.

A specific focus was placed on understanding whether there is a correlation between delivery methods and return rates. By exploring this relationship, the analysis sought to uncover insights into how different delivery approaches may influence the likelihood of returns during the Christmas sales period.
